About Me

I'm Kam Szeliga, a full-stack web developer.

My first exposure to coding was through BBCode on a forum way back in 2014. From there I ended up dabbling in a little bit of HTML here and there, mostly using it to make sure my artwork looked nice on personal web pages. I didn't do much with coding until recently. I decided to fully delve into the word of coding after figuring out that life in the very unpredictable animation industry wasn't going to work for me.

Front-end web development was always where I felt most comfortable, but after attending Coder Foundry I now find myself comfortable with not only front end development, but back end as well.
